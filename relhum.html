Sling Psychormeter

A sling psychrometer consists of a dry and wet-bulb thermometer, both containing mercury in the bulb portion of the glass tube. The dew point is the temperature at which water vapor condenses, while relative humidity is the ratio of the actual water vapor present in the air to the maximum amount the air can hold at a given temperature.

Hygrometer

This system uses an organic material (normally human hair) that expands and contracts as a result of the humidity around. This change can be made to move an indicator needle that moves across a scale, with graduations ranging from 0 to 100%.
